# Onboarding Process

The VBA onboarding process is designed for institutions that already maintain Bitcoin custody arrangements and want to deploy capital into pre-approved onchain venues without introducing a new custodial counterparty. The process accommodates Qualified Custodians, MPC-based custody networks, and self-custody setups.

{% hint style="info" %}
Before you start, make sure you read more about Verifiable Bitcoin Accounts via the [Threshold Institutional Page](https://www.threshold.network/institutional) and [VBA Release Notes](https://www.threshold.network/blog/verifiable-bitcoin-accounts).&#x20;
{% endhint %}

Timeline will depend on internal compliance review cycles and the complexity of the custody arrangement.

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Exploration and Qualification

We assess fit between your mandate and the VBA framework, covering custody preferences, target venues, and capital allocation parameters. This stage confirms alignment with your investment policy, regulatory posture, and operational requirements before any technical commitment is made.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Technical and Legal Briefing

Our team walks your investment, engineering, and legal stakeholders through the VBA architecture, the Bitcoin Script control logic, and the supporting contractual structure. Sessions are tailored by function, so technical, compliance, and treasury teams each receive the depth they require.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Validation and Process Review

Your security, risk, and compliance teams may conduct an independent review of the product design, custody integrations, and operational workflows. We provide documentation, code references, and direct engineering access so every control can be verified by internal stakeholders before approval.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### _(Optional) Account Control Agreement_

A tripartite agreement between your institution, the custodian, and Threshold Network Foundation establishes contractual enforceability of the onchain controls. This gives legal and audit teams a written instrument that mirrors the Bitcoin Script logic and codifies rights, obligations, and recovery procedures.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Test Transactions and Deployment

Once agreements are settled, Threshold will onboard your account for deployment. These provisions will enable you to execute end-to-end test transactions across the approved venues. This validates signing flows, recovery paths, and venue connectivity under live conditions before any production capital is committed.\
\
Capital is deployed into pre-approved venues such as Aave, Morpho, Curve, and Yield Basis according to the parameters defined in your account configuration. Every transaction is governed by the multi-party controls encoded in Bitcoin Script, with full visibility for your operations and treasury teams. Verifiable anytime using Bitcoin UTXO.

{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Support&#x20;

A dedicated **account manager** is assigned to your account for ongoing operations, monitoring, and incident response. We coordinate venue updates, performance reports, parameter adjustments, and periodic control reviews to keep the account aligned with your mandate over time.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

Every stage of VBA onboarding produces a verifiable artifact: a qualified mandate, a reviewed architecture, a signed agreement, a tested workflow. By execution, the framework governing your capital is fully understood, fully documented, and fully under your control.
